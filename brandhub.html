Usage Guidelines

How to use the brand correctly.

 Do

  • Use the logo on white, off-white or brand blue backgrounds
  • Keep clear space equal to the logo height on all sides
  • Use #4696e5 as the primary brand colour
  • Use the wordmark for formal documents and email signatures
  • Use the mark alone for social avatars and favicons
  • Maintain original aspect ratio at all times

 Don't

  • Recolour the logo to anything other than brand blue or white
  • Stretch, skew or distort the mark
  • Place the logo on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
  • Add drop shadows, outlines or effects to the mark
  • Use off-brand fonts alongside the logo
  • Scale below 24px — use favicon at small sizes
